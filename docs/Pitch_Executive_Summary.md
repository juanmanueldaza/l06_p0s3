# Pitch Deck Executive Summary

**Journalism doesn't need another CMS; it needs a Studio.**

l06_p0s3 (Log Pose) is the first "Newsroom Operating System" designed as a creative instrument—an "Ableton Live for News"—that orchestrates AI agents to handle the drudgery of sourcing and drafting while keeping the journalist in total creative control. Like the Log Pose from One Piece that locks onto the next island and guides navigators through unpredictable seas, l06_p0s3 locks onto the story and guides journalists through the chaos of information. Unlike "black box" generators that hallucinate, our **Human-in-the-Loop** architecture enforces strict provenance, visually linking every claim to a verified source to solve the trust crisis in modern media. Built on enterprise-grade security (OWASP/NIST), we offer the speed of automation with the integrity of traditional reporting. This is not just a tool for managing content; it is an exoskeleton for the investigative mind.
